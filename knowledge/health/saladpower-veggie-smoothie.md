# SaladPower - Veggie Smoothie

*Website: https://www.saladpower.com/*

## What It Is

USDA Certified Organic smoothie that delivers **2x your daily vegetable requirement** in every pouch.

## Ingredients

- Spinach
- Kale
- Carrot
- Broccoli
- Apple
- Lemon

Nothing more, nothing less. Quantities based on average weights from most recent harvest.

## Why One Flavor?

Developed to maximize vegetable nutrition density while minimizing sugar. Geared towards functionality (and still tastes great).

## How to Drink

- Anytime you think you need more veggies
- Tastes best chilled
- Can be stored at room temperature thanks to pouch seal technology
- Once cap is twisted open, consume within 4 hours for maximum nutritional benefit

## Use Case

Quick way to get vegetables when you don't have time for salads or cooking.
