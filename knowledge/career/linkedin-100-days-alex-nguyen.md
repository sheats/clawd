## Key Takeaways

### The Challenge
- Introvert who felt shy about sharing thoughts
- Committed to posting on LinkedIn for 100 consecutive days
- Made it public by numbering each post (accountability)
- Started as unemployed university student

### Why It Worked
- **Social peer pressure** ‚Äî telling everyone creates accountability
- **Consistency beats perfection** ‚Äî just showing up daily matters
- **Compound effect** ‚Äî Day 1 is scary, Day 100 is second nature

### The Result
- Never has to worry about finding a job
- Network comes to him
- Established as a thought leader in his space
