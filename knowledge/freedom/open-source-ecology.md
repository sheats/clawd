# Open Source Ecology

**Source:** https://www.opensourceecology.org/
**Saved:** 2026-01-25

## Overview

Open Source Blueprints for Civilization. Build Yourself.

Open Source Ecology develops open source industrial machines that can be made for a fraction of commercial costs, sharing designs online for free. The goal is to create an open source economy – an efficient economy which increases innovation by open collaboration.

See the [Global Village Construction Set TED Talk](https://www.ted.com/talks/marcin_jakubowski?language=en) for an overview.

## Future Builders Academy

An alternative to college or mid-life correction. Become a future builder, builder of the future, and build your own future.

**Focus areas:**
- Literacy, numeracy, oracy
- Emotional literacy and moral literacy
- Interdisciplinary, complex systems learning
- Learning how to learn, think, and be human
- Practice by doing, learn by teaching

**Programs:**
- 2 month integrated design trimester
- 4-year Fellowship

[More info](https://www.opensourceecology.org/ready-to-re-design-civilization/)

## Builder Crash Course – Sep 1-14, 2025

Complete build of a modular, 800 square foot, 2 bed 2 bath starter home from foundation to interior finish.

**What you learn:**
- Every step from foundation to interior finish and utilities
- 6 kW of rooftop photovoltaics installation
- Design iterations in Sweet Home 3D
- Technical files manipulation in FreeCAD
- Optimized water, plumbing, electrical, and heat pump installation
- Building systems into modular wall units
- Thermal battery and geothermal cooling for extremely high efficiency
- Meeting over 90% of building's electrical needs using photovoltaic energy

[More info](https://www.opensourceecology.org/builder_crash_course_2025/)

## Build Your Own House in 6 Weeks – Sep 15 – Oct 31, 2025

Build your own 800 square foot, expandable starter home transported on a 40' trailer.

**Cost:** ~$25k-$50k of materials
**Spots:** 6 teams
**Participation:** Free in founding cohort (by application only)

[More info](https://www.opensourceecology.org/financial_independence_package/)
