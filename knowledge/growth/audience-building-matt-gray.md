## The Content Waterfall System

Turn 1 single thread into:
- 5 tweets
- 2 TikTok videos
- 2 YouTube Shorts
- 2 Instagram Reels
- 1 LinkedIn Carousel
- 1 Instagram Carousel
- 1 newsletter (in 6 weeks)

**Stop rewriting content.**

## Platform Strategies

### Instagram (87K followers/month)
- **Secret weapon:** Animated reels
- These always blow up
- Get on them before it's too late

### Twitter/X
- Write threads
- Squeeze content into multiple formats

### Vertical Video Flywheel
Works for:
- TikTok
- YouTube Shorts
- Instagram Reels

Great for driving newsletter signups.

### YouTube Long Form
- Build deeper relationships
- Share tips, insights, frameworks
- Drive people to newsletter
- Monetize

### LinkedIn Carousels
*"Carousels are the reels of LinkedIn"*

How to make:
1. Find a high-performing thread
2. Spice up design with Canva
3. Create images from it on TweetPik

### Newsletter (ConvertKit)
**Why you need to own your audience:**
- Automated sequences
- Email segmentation
- Landing pages

### Podcast
- Deepens relationships
- Interview top founders
- Get free mentorship
- Build goodwill
