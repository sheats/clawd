# AI Email Personalization Tools for High Cotton

*Research: January 28, 2026*

---

## The Goal
Kill mass emails. Use AI to tailor emails individually based on order history, browsing behavior, and customer profile.

---

## Tier 1: Full Email Platforms with AI

### Klaviyo (Current Standard)
- **What it does:** Deep Shopify integration, AI-powered segmentation, predictive analytics, product recommendations
- **AI features:** Predictive send times, churn prediction, product recommendations in emails
- **Pricing:** Starts ~$45/month for 1,500 contacts, scales up quickly
- **Verdict:** Good but expensive. Does basic personalization well.

### Omnisend
- **What it does:** Email + SMS + push notifications, built for e-commerce
- **AI features:** Smart segmentation, product recommendations, campaign automation
- **Pricing:** Free up to 250 contacts, paid starts ~$16/month
- **Verdict:** Cheaper Klaviyo alternative, good for smaller brands

### ActiveCampaign
- **What it does:** Advanced automation, CRM built-in, lead scoring
- **AI features:** AI-powered workflows, predictive sending, content generation
- **Pricing:** Starts ~$29/month
- **Verdict:** Best for complex automation, steeper learning curve

### Drip
- **What it does:** "E-commerce CRM" - built specifically for online brands
- **AI features:** Behavior-based automation, personalized product recommendations
- **Pricing:** Starts ~$39/month for 2,500 contacts
- **Verdict:** Good middle ground between Klaviyo and cheaper options

---

## Tier 2: AI-First / Hyper-Personalization Tools

### RetainIQ
- **What it does:** Hyper-personalized emails & SMS for e-commerce
- **Key feature:** 1:1 personalization at scale - different email content for each recipient
- **Shopify App:** Yes
- **Verdict:** ⭐ This is closest to Peter's vision — truly individualized emails

### Retain AI (Shopify App)
- **What it does:** AI-generated winback emails
- **Key feature:** 90 days after purchase, sends personalized re-engagement
- **Pricing:** Check Shopify app store
- **Verdict:** Simple, focused tool for retention specifically

### Rebuy Personalization Engine
- **What it does:** AI-powered upsells, cross-sells, smart product discovery
- **Key feature:** Recommends products at checkout based on behavior
- **Shopify App:** Yes, highly rated
- **Verdict:** More for on-site personalization than email, but can feed email recommendations

---

## Tier 3: Budget Options

### Sender
- **What it does:** Email + SMS marketing
- **Pricing:** 79% cheaper than Klaviyo, 24/7 support
- **Verdict:** Good if budget is primary concern

### Brevo (formerly Sendinblue)
- **What it does:** Pay-per-email pricing
- **Pricing:** Starts $12/month with 100,000 contacts
- **Verdict:** Cheapest for large lists, basic personalization

---

## Recommended Stack for High Cotton

**Option A: Simple (Start Here)**
1. **Klaviyo** (or Omnisend if budget-conscious)
2. Use built-in AI features for segmentation + product recs
3. Set up flows: welcome, post-purchase, winback, browse abandonment
4. ~$50-100/month

**Option B: Advanced (Peter's Vision)**
1. **Klaviyo** for email infrastructure
2. **RetainIQ** layer on top for 1:1 hyper-personalization
3. **Rebuy** for on-site product recommendations (feeds into email data)
4. ~$150-300/month total

**Option C: DIY with AI**
1. Basic email platform (Omnisend/Sender)
2. Build custom AI layer using Claude/GPT API
3. Pull order history → generate personalized copy per customer
4. More work but most flexible and cheapest long-term
