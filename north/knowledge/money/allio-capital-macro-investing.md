# Allio Capital - AI-Powered Macro Investing Platform

Website: [alliocapital.com](https://www.alliocapital.com/)

## What It Is

Allio is an investment platform focused on **macro investing** - building portfolios based on big economic forces (inflation, interest rates, global trends) rather than individual stock picking.

## Key Features

- **ALTITUDE AI™** - Proprietary AI that integrates predictive models and macroeconomic data
- Portfolio construction guidance
- Risk management
- Real-time market adaptability
- Tailored investing experience

## Philosophy

> "Economic literacy is more than numbers—it's the key to unlocking opportunity, independence, and lasting prosperity."

Focus is on understanding how economies work to make smarter decisions and build resilient portfolios.
