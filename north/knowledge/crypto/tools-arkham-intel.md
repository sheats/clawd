# Arkham Intelligence

URL: https://x.com/ArkhamIntel
Website: https://platform.arkhamintelligence.com/

## What It Is
Blockchain analytics platform that de-anonymizes blockchain activity. Tracks wallets, entities, and fund flows across chains.

## Use Cases
- Track whale wallets and institutional movements
- Identify exchange wallets and fund flows
- Research token holder distributions
- Monitor smart money movements
- Due diligence on projects

## Notes
- Has intel exchange for buying/selling wallet information
- Popular for tracking large holders and VC wallets
- Good for spotting accumulation/distribution patterns
