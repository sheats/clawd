## Overview
Ashwagandha is one of the most important herbs in Ayurveda. "Ashwagandha" is Sanskrit for "smell of the horse" - refers to both the herb's scent and its potential to increase strength.

## Proven Benefits

### 1. Reduces Stress
- Classified as an adaptogen (helps body cope with stress)
- Controls stress mediators including cortisol
- 250-600mg daily for 8 weeks showed significant reduction in perceived stress
- Improves sleep quality

### 2. Enhances Athletic Performance
- Improves strength and oxygen use during exercise
- Enhances VO2 max (heart and lung fitness)
- 600mg daily + resistance training = greater muscle strength and size gains

### 3. May Help Depression/Anxiety
- 1,000mg daily showed reductions in depression and anxiety
- May help manage insomnia and mental health issues
- More research needed

### 4. Boosts Testosterone
- 18% greater increase in DHEA-S (sex hormone)
- 14.7% greater increase in testosterone vs placebo
- Increased sperm concentration and motility

### 5. May Lower Blood Sugar
- Withaferin A has antidiabetic activity
- Helps stimulate cells to absorb glucose

### 6. Reduces Inflammation
- Reduces inflammatory markers like IL-10

## Recommended Forms
- KSM-66 (full-spectrum root extract) is popular
- Dosages typically 250-600mg daily
